Shonen Genre

Is a genre aimed at young male readers, featuring action driven stories of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth through determined protagonists.

Seinen Genre

Is a genre aimed at adult male readers, featuring more mature themes and complex storytelling.

Shojo Genre

Is a genre aimed at young female readers, focusing on romance, emotions, and personal relationships, often told through expressive art and character driven stories.

Josei Genre

Is a genre aimed at adult female readers, featuring more mature themes and complex storytelling.
