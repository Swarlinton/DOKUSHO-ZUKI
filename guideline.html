User Guidelines

Learn how to use DOKUSHO-ZUKI responsibly and get the most out of your experience.

About DOKUSHO-ZUKI

DOKUSHO-ZUKI is a community-driven manga library and reading platform. Our mission is to provide manga enthusiasts with a space to discover, read, and discuss their favorite titles while respecting copyright and community standards.

Community Conduct

We maintain a welcoming and respectful environment for all users. Please adhere to these guidelines:

  • Be respectful and courteous to other users and their opinions
  • Avoid harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
  • Do not spam or flood the community with repetitive content
  • Respect the intellectual property rights of manga creators and publishers
  • Keep discussions relevant to manga, anime, and related topics
  • Report any inappropriate content or behavior to our moderation team

Content Policy

To maintain a safe and appropriate community, the following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Explicit adult content or pornography
  • Pirated or illegally distributed manga
  • Copyright infringing material without proper licensing
  • Violent, graphic, or disturbing content (unless within context of manga reviews)
  • Personal information disclosure of other users
  • Malicious links or phishing attempts

⚠️ Violations of these policies may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from the platform.

Forum Etiquette

When posting in our community forums, please:

  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your posts
  • Search existing discussions before posting duplicate topics
  • Use appropriate category tags (Shōnen, Shōjo, Seinen, Josei, etc.)
  • Provide context and examples when asking for recommendations
  • Mark spoilers with warning labels before revealing plot details
  • Give constructive criticism and praise creators' hard work

Reading History & Favorites

Our platform tracks your reading progress to enhance your experience:

  • Your reading history is stored locally on your device by default
  • You can view your reading history in your Profile section
  • Clear your history at any time from your account settings
  • Use the "Continue Reading" feature to resume from where you left off
  • Add manga to your Favorites to quickly access your preferred titles

Privacy & Account Security

Your privacy is important to us. Please:

  • Never share your password with anyone, including staff members
  • Use a unique password for your DOKUSHO-ZUKI account
  • Log out when using shared devices
  • Keep your email address up to date
  • Report suspicious account activity immediately
  • Review our Privacy Policy for detailed information on data handling

Best Practices

To get the best experience from DOKUSHO-ZUKI:

  • Complete your profile with accurate information
  • Explore different genres and discover new manga
  • Engage with the community through discussions and recommendations
  • Leave reviews and ratings to help other readers
  • Follow favorite authors and series to stay updated
  • Report content that needs licensing review to our team

Support & Questions

If you have questions about these guidelines or need assistance, please:

  • Visit the Contact Us page
  • Check the About Us section for more information
  • Reach out to our community moderators in the forums
  • Review our homepage for additional resources

ℹ️ These guidelines are subject to change. Please review them periodically for updates.
